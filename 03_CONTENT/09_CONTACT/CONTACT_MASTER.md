## Purpose
The Contact page gives visitors a clear and aligned way to reach New Earth.

Its role is to:
- invite meaningful contact
- support collaboration and aligned inquiry
- create a practical next step for interested visitors
- keep communication calm and focused
- avoid clutter or overly sales-driven tone

---

## Core page message

New Earth welcomes thoughtful contact from people who resonate with the vision, the Blueprint, the systems direction, or the practical implementation path through MicroGrow.

The Contact page is an invitation to communicate clearly and purposefully.

---

## Tone

The page should feel:
- open
- calm
- clear
- respectful
- intentional

It should avoid:
- aggressive lead capture tone
- vague “reach out anytime for anything” language
- clutter
- too many competing contact routes
